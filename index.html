Stanford Pre-Business Association

Mission
SPBA is Stanford’s all-encompassing business organization. We provide our members exposure to three core pillars -- marketing, consulting, and finance -- and help them develop as businessmen and businesswomen through real-world project-based work, leadership experience, internal knowledge-sharing, and campus-wide events with today’s most promising companies.
Constitution
SPBA prides itself on having a high performing yet inclusive team environment. We expect our members to collaborate with one another and maintain the utmost integrity by promoting fellow teammates. SPBA’s internal culture is as supportive as the business world is competitive, as growth and development within the team and between members is necessary as each individual prepares to become a leader in the near future.
